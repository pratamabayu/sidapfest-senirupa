SIDAPFEST 2022

The Department of Fine Arts, Universitas Negeri Semarang, as a tertiary institution that actively holds exhibitions at the international level, tries to play a role in the discourse of future thinking. The SIDAPFEST exhibition was then created to increase public awareness through digital painting education after recovering from Covid-19 . Now SIDAPFEST (Semarang International Digital Art Painting) carries a theme, namely thinking about the future with the title visionary.

Your imagination is the vehicle for the attractions of your future life. Albert Einstein

Visionary refers to someone who has a paradigm or view of the future, has innovation with realistic planning and is able to lead to a clear vision. The visionary's role is to express creative ideas and ideas that aim to realize their abilities so that they are able to implement and find solutions to environmental problems, the development of science, technology, automotive, or anticipate changes that occur in themselves and the world as improvements and have an impact on change. Let's go to the future together.
